# decent.church - Site Instructions
# This site provides Scripture-grounded reflections for finding faith, church family, purpose, and neighbor love.

# Site Purpose
Purpose: A welcoming Judeo-Christian resource centered on loving God and loving neighbor.

# Content Structure
Content-Type: Scripture-based articles and reflection guides
Topics: Finding a church family, finding faith, discerning purpose, healthy congregations, prayer, Scripture, service, community
Perspective: Broadly welcoming to Judeo-Christian values, rooted in the greatest commandments
Bible-Translation: NIV
Difficulty-Levels: Starting Out, Growing, Going Deeper
Format: Markdown-based articles with Scripture foundations, reflection questions, practices, prayer, key takeaways, and further reading

# Machine-Readable Endpoints
API-Guides: /api/guides.json
API-Series: /api/series.json
API-Topics: /api/topics.json
API-Index: /index.json
Feed: /feed.xml
Sitemap: /sitemap.xml

# Content Organization
- Articles can be standalone or part of a series
- Series are multi-part learning paths on faith, community, purpose, and healthy church life
- Each article includes a path level, estimated reading time, tags, and optional prerequisites
- Content should avoid emojis and keep a warm, pastoral tone
